Overview

Play the classic Monopoly online. This free online version of Monopoly is just like the original board game that we all know and love. Roll the dice and move around the board, Land on properties to buy them or auction them off to other players. Collect rent when other players land on your properties. Build houses and hotels on complete blocks of property to increase the value in them. Trade other players and make a deal to take over more properties. The game is over when only one player remains after all of the other players have gone bankrupt.
